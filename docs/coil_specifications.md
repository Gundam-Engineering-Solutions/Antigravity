# Technical Specification: 4-Layer Bitter-Style Coil Stack

## 1. Design & Core Geometry
To compete effectively with the high pressure profiles of industrial -12 hydraulic lines, maximum magnetic flux density ($B$) must be concentrated uniformly inside the actuator envelope. 
* **Actuator Style:** Solenoid-based cylindrical configuration optimized for massive, high-density linear holding force and rapid acceleration vectors.
* **Core Material:** High-permeability soft iron core components nested tightly to maximize magnetic saturation thresholds.

## 2. Winding Schedule (48V/5A Optimization)
The physical dimensions of the coil layers are mathematically mapped to the -12 dash size profile to ensure structural fill factor optimization:

* **Conductor Type:** 14 AWG Equivalent Square-Section Enameled Copper Wire. The square cross-section maximizes the copper fill factor to 90-95%, completely eliminating the performance-degrading air gaps found in standard round wire windings.
* **Insulation Coating:** Grade 2 Polyester-imide (Kapton equivalent) designed to withstand high thermal shock and ozone exposure without dielectric breakdown.
* **Bore Inner Diameter (ID):** 19.05 mm (0.75 in) — matching standard -12 fitting parameters.
* **Winding Total Length:** 150 mm.
* **Layer Depth:** 4 Concentric Layers.
* **Turns per Layer:** 80 Turns.
* **Total Turn Count ($N$):** 320 Turns.
* **Calculated Circuit Resistance ($R$):** ~9.1 Ohms.
* **Magnetic Strength Factor:** 1,600 Ampere-turns.

## 3. Structural Reinforcement
* **Layer Separation:** 0.05 mm Nomex/Aramid insulation sheets inserted between each of the 4 layers to prevent voltage breakdown and cushion against the high mechanical "hammer effect" generated by the rapid magnetic pulse.
* **Thermal Management:** 0.5 mm vertical spacers integrated every 20 turns to define active vane-axial flow paths, allowing high-static-pressure 48V fans to pass dry air directly through the core windings to prevent copper heat soak.
